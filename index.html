Saraswat Bank's Internet Banking is simple, secured & safe net banking platform for managing the accounts online. Wide range of services & facilities are available online with the ease of access!

Internet Banking can be accessed at Desktop/ Laptop/ Tablet & even at Mobile handsets. Saraswat Bank Internet Banking lets you check your balance, statements, manage your term deposits & loans, bill payments & many more.

Everything you need to do with your bank account using Online Banking at your convenience in a fast and secure manner.

First Time Login Made Easy!

Self Onboarding: Register today and logon to Internet Banking to experience new-aged banking. Register yourself instantly with your Saraswat Bank Debit Card. Just enter your Debit Card details and validate it through OTP sent to your registered mobile number.

Features:

  • Account Overview: View saving/ current/ overdraft bank account balance, details and download statements.
  • Reset Password: Reset Password using Debit Card.
  • Bill Payments: Make your monthly bill payments & recharges online easily.
  • Loans: View loan account details at a glance & get statements on your registered email.
  • Fixed Deposits: View instantly & open Fixed Deposits and Recurring Deposits.
  • Fund Transfer:
    • Intra Bank: Transfer funds to any other accounts of Saraswat Bank.
    • Inter Bank Funds Transfer (RTGS/NEFT/IMPS): Transfer funds to other bank accounts.
  • E-Services:
    • Manage Limits
    • View tax credit details.
  • Cheques:
    • Give stop payment instructions on issued cheques
    • Request for Cheque Book
    • Cheque status request
